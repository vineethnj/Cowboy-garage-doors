Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of our most commonly asked questions about garage doors and our services.

How long does a garage door installation take?

A standard garage door installation typically takes 4-6 hours, depending on the complexity of the job and whether old door removal is required. Our technicians work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine while ensuring quality installation.

What types of garage door materials do you offer?

We offer garage doors in a variety of materials including steel, wood, aluminum, fiberglass, and vinyl. Each material has unique benefits in terms of appearance, insulation, durability, and maintenance requirements. Our team can help you select the best option for your specific needs and aesthetic preferences.

How often should garage door maintenance be performed?

We recommend professional garage door maintenance at least once a year to ensure safe and reliable operation. However, commercial doors or those with heavy usage may require more frequent service. Regular maintenance extends the life of your door and can prevent costly emergency repairs in the future.

Do you offer warranties on your services?

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. New garage door installations typically come with a manufacturer's warranty on parts (varying by manufacturer) and our own labor warranty. Repair services are also backed by a warranty on both parts and labor. We'll provide full warranty details before any work begins.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency garage door issue?

For emergency situations, we offer same-day service in most cases. Our technicians are dispatched quickly to address urgent issues like doors that won't close or severe damage. We maintain fully-stocked service vehicles to complete most repairs in a single visit, minimizing downtime and inconvenience.
